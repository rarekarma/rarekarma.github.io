Salesforce Development Lead

Competitive Salary plus Equity

We are a startup Salesforce consulting firm, looking for an experienced Salesforce Developer to help us build and grow our business. We focus on custom development and integration on the Salesforce platform.

You will be the technical lead on client projects and hire a talented team of Salesforce developers to support you and our clients.

In addition to a competitive salary, you will receive a meaningful equity stake in the business.

We are looking for someone who has the following qualifications and experience:

  • An entrepreneurial mindset. You like to start new things and get excited by the opportunity to build a team and a business from scratch.
  • An active Salesforce Platform Developer I and Developer II certification. Additional certifications are a plus.
  • 5+ years' of experience with designing and implementing custom solutions on the Salesforce platform.
  • Experience in designing the Salesforce Object Model, Security Model, SOQL as well as configuring the Salesforce Lightning Experience.
  • Experience in Salesforce application development using Apex and advanced techniques like Batch Apex, Caching, Callouts etc.
  • Experience in Salesforce UI development using Visualforce, Lightning Aura and Web Components along with basic web development skills like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • Experience with integrating with the Salesforce platform (inbound/outbound) using the REST/SOAP APIs, MuleSoft, or Data Loader.
  • Experience in using Salesforce development tools like VS Code.
  • Experience in implementing Sales Cloud or Service Cloud as well as having a basic understanding of the Salesforce ecosystem including Einstein, Experience cloud, Marketing cloud etc.
  • Experience working in Agile/Scrum environment with remote team members
  • Experience working with offshore teams.
